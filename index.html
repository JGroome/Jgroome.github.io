Problem statement: Sports TV

Sport lovers need a easier way to determine which games will be on their location TV channels or a way to see how they can watch their wanted game if it is not playing in their location.

Affinity diagram: Sports app

With the app sports fans can select interested sports put in their region for thoes sports, get local information for the sports they chose and have an option to favorite sports and see their merch.

Team Personas: Where to watch sports

My persona (Zach Rodgers) is meant to be you basic football fan who isnt very techinically inclined but know hot to use a phone at least. "I love Football!"

Team Storyboards: Uses of the app

A man named Zach needs a way to find his favorite sport team and where he can watch it, he uses our app in order to find his team.

Team Sketches: Team Favoriting

Select a sport you want (one is favorited which is why it shows up at the top) shows current games going on and then if you tap on a game it gives more information and link to each team page and where you can watch the game.
